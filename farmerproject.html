Project Overview

This platform is designed to empower small and mid-scale farmers—especially tea and agri-farmers—by connecting them directly with buyers, eliminating middlemen, and ensuring fair pricing and transparency.

Core features will include:

  • Direct farm-to-buyer trading
  • Logistics and transport integration
  • Product certification and traceability
  • AI-based pricing suggestions
  • Low-code tools for farmers to list their produce easily
